Tic Tac Toe Arena

Tic Tac Toe Arena is a browser game built for quick strategy matches. It supports solo practice, two-player local games, and online rooms for friends who want to play from different devices.

What you can play

Choose computer mode for practice, single-device mode for a local match, or multiplayer mode to create and join a private room.

How scoring works

The app tracks wins during a session. You can restart the board or reset the score when you want a fresh match.

Our approach

The game is lightweight, free to use, and designed to work on common desktop and mobile browsers.

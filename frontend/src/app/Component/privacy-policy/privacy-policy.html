Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Tic Tac Toe Arena handles information when you use the game. Last updated: May 25, 2026.

Information we use

The game may use player names, room IDs, game moves, scores, and browser connection details to run matches and keep the game synchronized.

Advertising

We may use Google AdSense to show ads. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, device identifiers, and similar technologies to deliver, measure, and personalize ads.

Cookies and choices

You can manage cookies in your browser settings. You can also learn about Google ad personalization choices at Google Advertising Policies.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Service providers may process limited technical data only to host the game, run multiplayer features, measure performance, or display ads.

Children

The game is intended for a general audience. If you believe a child has shared personal information, contact us so we can review and remove it where required.

Contact

For privacy questions, email contact@tictactoearena.com.
